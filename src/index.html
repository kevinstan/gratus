Why Gratus?

Gratus is reinventing charitable funding for people and organizations in need of support via decentralization. Our non-profit platform makes the donating process a rewardable, educational and interactive experience, by providing donors with special ERC721 coEasy to Use & Customizellectibles that our "creators" make when ETH is donated. Smart contract programming should be made available to everyone!

How does Gratus work?

"Creators", people and organizations in need of funding and support, will learn how to create and deploy a simple smart contract with all their relevant information. "Donors", people willing and interested in supporting creators, will receive a special collectible(s) created by the "Creators" in exchange for support. This entire process is built on the Ethereum Blockchain, which removes bureacracy and enables complete transparency and proof of ownership of the collectible(s).
